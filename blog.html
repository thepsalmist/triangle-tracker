Triangles

Triangles are two-dimensional shapes made up of three sides and three angles. Though not all of them are the same, they all have to have those two elements in place in order to be a triangle. You’ll run across several different types, each one defined by its sides and/or angles.

  1. Acute: In an acute triangle all three angles are less than 90 degrees
  2. Obtuse:Obtuse triangles have one angle measuring more than 90 degrees
  3. Right:A right triangle has one 90 degree angle
  4. Equilateral:Equilateral triangles have three equal sides and three equal angles
  5. Scalene:With a scalene triangle, you’ll have no equal sides or angles
  6. Isosceles:Isosceles triangles have two equal sides and two equal angles
